Atlanta Falcons 2016 Season

The 2016 Atlanta Falcons were one of the most prolific NFL offenses of this century. The team ended up losing in a memorable fashion in the Super Bowl.

The Falcons got off to a rough start losing at home to the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, but they recovered by winning four straight games. After dropping two in a row, they strung togther an 11-5 record. What was most impressive was the offensive numbers that the Falcons were able to produce. They scored 33.8 points per game, which ranked 8th of all time in NFL history.

The Playoffs

The Falcons got off to a roaring start in the playoffs. They won their first two playoff games, but they ended up blowing a 28-3 lead in the Super Bowl to the New England Patriots.
